Type Is Never Still.

Kinetic Type treats the letterform not as a passive vessel for language, but as a charged particle. Each character carries velocity, memory, and intention. Words don't simply appear — they ARRIVE.

This system defines typography as choreography. The Bebas Neue display face cuts like a press plate — sharp, industrial, deliberate. Instrument Serif breathes beneath it, adding the warmth of a hand-composed paragraph. Together they perform.

The page is ALIVE. Nothing rests. Hover anything. Scroll everything. Watch.
